Mr. Farmer Program

/ Mr. Farmer Program

Promoting Environment Sustainability

Project Name: Promoting Environment Sustainability and Enabling Farmers for Climate smart practices

Location: Bangalore Urban and Rural

Period: 3 to 4 Months

Implementing Partner: Mantarlee Social Innovations Federation

Support Amount: Rs 45,000 each farmer/-

Project Brief

According to the 2011 Census, about 86% of farmers in India are small and marginal. Improving their status requires a multifaceted approach that addresses financial, educational, technological, and social needs.

Climate Smart Agriculture (CSA) is an integrated approach to managing cropland, livestock, and forests to address the interlinked challenges of food security and climate change. CSA aims to achieve three outcomes: Increased Productivity, Enhanced Resilience, and Reduced Emissions.

We have the infrastructure facility to train farmers in climate-smart best practices such as:

  • Improving yield through vermi composting
  • Using Bee Boxes
  • Applying BioChar to improve soil quality and reduce carbon
  • Promoting agroforestry to increase farmer income

We are now focused on leveraging this facility to enable farmers to implement these best practices by providing:

  • Vermi composting kits
  • BioChar production units
  • Bee Boxes
  • Native fruit plants such as Lemon, Tamarind, and Coconut

Upskilling farmers: Implementing training programs, workshops, and extension services to educate farmers on best practices and innovations through state and central agriculture/horticulture institutions like IIHR and KVKs.

Government Support and Policies: Many farmers are unaware of government schemes and policies designed to support them. Raising awareness about relevant schemes and entitlements can significantly benefit farmers.

Key objectives

  1. To increase income of farmers.
  2. To promote climate smart agriculture by improving infrastructure available to the farmers.
  3. Environment conservation by way of Agroforestry & increasing soil fertility.
  4. Awareness on government programs.

Deliverable/Outcome

  1. Installation and training to 20 - 100 farmers for vermi composting, Bee Box and BioChar production, Horticulture, Banana Plantation, Dairy and Agroforestry - 50 saplings for each farmer.
  2. Awareness of PMJJBY ( Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana ) etc.
  3. Training will be given by Govt Authorities ( KVK ) and AnantGram Team at AnantGram on CSA.

Qualitative Impact

This will be a very effective model of training, empowering and enabling farmers on various environment aspects. This will enable farmers to implement climate smart agriculture and at the same time improving their livelihood

Quantitative Impact

20 to 100 farmers trained, empowered and enabled to implement vermi composting, Bee Box, BioChar production and agroforestry with 50 fruit plants.

Mr. Farmer Program

Empowering Small & Marginal Farmers with Climate Smart Practices

The “Mr. Farmer” Program empowers small and marginal farmers through Climate Smart Agriculture (CSA). We offer training, tools, and support to promote practices like vermi composting, bee boxes, biochar use, and agroforestry, in collaboration with institutions like KVKs and IIHR.
