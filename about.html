Our Story

They say a journey of thousand miles starts from a single step. How true it is..!!!

Ours started over a cup of coffee with a simple idea of creating an impact on the social landscape… We debated anything and everything under the Sun to see what is possible and what works. We explored technology, proposal, fund-raising, baseline survey, impact report, CSR, education, skill development, healthcare, regenerative farming, women empowerment, environment, tree plantation and what not? And one day we discovered a pattern is emerging out of nowhere we could see a method in madness, a sense in non sense, a purpose in cluttered efforts, little clarity in uncertainty.

Colleagues can be friends and can also become partners. And that’s exHCLite spirit of entrepreneurship.

Our Mission

To create wealth and respect for farmers, inspire ecological stewardship among children, and nurture innovation in education.

Why It Matters

  • India ranks 117th in Global Hunger Index and 199th in Human Development Index.
  • Marginal farmers are dependent on uncertain rainfall and often fall into debt traps.
  • The next generation needs both ecological awareness and innovative skills.

Our Approach

We work closely with grassroots NGOs, educators, and local communities to ensure every intervention is practical, scalable, and sustainable. Through hands-on training, model farms, environmental education, and innovation labs, we bring meaningful change.
