Mr. Farmer Initiative

Empowering Farmers with Climate-Smart Solutions

As part of the Mr. Farmer initiative, we empower small and marginal farmers with sustainable agricultural tools and knowledge. We offer climate-smart kits such as Active Charcoal, Vermi-Composting, Agroforestry, Bee Boxes, and Organic Pest Repellent Solutions along with setup, training, and continuous support.

Our Mission

Empowering Farmers with Sustainable Solutions

Our mission is to support small and marginal farmers by providing tools, knowledge, and training to embrace sustainable agricultural practices and improve their livelihoods.

Active Charcoal

Introduce farmers to Active Charcoal as a soil amendment to improve soil health and increase crop productivity.

Vermi-Composting

Earthworms enhance soil structure and detoxify the environment, making them natural engineers for better soil quality.

Agroforestry

Agroforestry integrates trees into farming systems to improve soil health and provide income from fruit-bearing trees.

Bee Boxes

Beekeeping can increase crop yields through pollination and provide extra income from honey harvesting.
