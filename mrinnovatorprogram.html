Mr. Innovator Program

/ Mr. Innovator Program

Innovation Center for Applied Education

Project Name: Innovation Center for Applied Education

Location: Bangalore Urban and Rural

Period: 1 year

Implementing Partner: Mantarlee Social Innovations Federation

Support Amount: ₹30 lakhs

Project Brief

To tackle the challenges of student employability, the Mantarlee Program proposes the creation of an Applied Innovations Lab. This initiative aims to provide students with hands-on experience in emerging technologies such as:

  • Robotics
  • 3D Printing
  • Internet of Things (IoT)
  • Data Science

Additionally, the lab will feature an AI-powered Learning Management System (LMS) to enhance personalized learning and track progress effectively. The overall goal is to equip students with practical skills and innovation capabilities that align with industry demands, ensuring they are job-ready upon graduation.

Key objectives

  1. Set up an applied innovations lab.
  2. Foster a culture of innovation among faculty and students.
  3. Train 500 students.
  4. Explore industrial collaboration.

Deliverable/Outcome

  1. Establishment of a state-of-the-art applied innovations lab.
  2. Training and exposure for 10 faculty members and 500 students.
  3. Creation of model use cases and prototypes.
  4. Establishment of industrial collaborations.

Qualitative Impact

  • A state-of-the-art facility that promotes innovation and collaboration.
  • Students develop transformative solutions, potentially leading to patents and startups.

Quantitative Impact

  • 1 facility supported for training.
  • 10 faculties trained.
  • Exposure provided to 500 students.
  • Collaboration with 5 industries.
